Our Story

We're makers who got tired of making do

The Guilded Gauge grew out of frustration — and a love for artisan communities that deserve better software. We spend time in every craft before we write a line of code. The quirks, the terminology, the workarounds nobody should have to make.

Good tools feel like they were made by someone who gets it. That's what we're always chasing — software that fits into a maker's world, not the other way around.
